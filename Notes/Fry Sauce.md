**Ingredients**

- 1/2 cup mayo
- 1/3 cup ketchup
- 1 tbsp mustard
- 1 tsp barbecue sauce
- 1 tsp white vinegar

**Instructions**

1. Combine all ingredients in a small bowl.
2. Whisk until smooth with no lumps.
3. Serve the with french fries or on hamburgers / sandwiches.
